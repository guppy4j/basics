Basics of life : Be good

  • Be respectful, patient and kind with others and with yourself.
  • Preserve and protect our natural environment and fellow creatures.
  • Live sustainably. Call out and boycott unsustainable practices.
  • Resist violence and violations non-violently.
  • Protect and support those in need.
  • Listen carefully and try to understand.
  • Negotiate and compromise to achieve fairness.
  • Include everyone affected in decision-making processes.
  • Be honest and only promise what you can keep.
  • Establish a sustainable sharing economy.
  • Establish agreed-upon limits on private property.
  • Control means of production collectively and democratically.
  • Keep rules and tools simple and accessible for everyone.
  • Focus and work on solutions, not complaints and blame.

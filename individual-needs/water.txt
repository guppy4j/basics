- Find natural water source (river, rain, dew, ocean, etc)

If water is not clean
- Filter (clean sand, coffee filter, reusable filter)
- Sanitize (PET bottles, tinfoil, sun)
-> SODIS = Solar Disinfection : http://www.sodis.ch/
- Desalienate if necessary:
  https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solare_Meerwasserentsalzung#Einfache_solare_Verdunstungsanlagen

- Fill containers with clean water
